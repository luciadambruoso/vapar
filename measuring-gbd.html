Measuring global burden of disease: adding value to verbal autopsy

Professor Peter Byass, Umeå University. The causes of half the world’s deaths pass unrecorded by routine registration systems, particularly in low- and middle-income countries. Verbal autopsy collects information from witnesses of a death to assign medical causes; this talk presented developments in VA methods. Peter was Professor of Global Health at Umeå University and Honorary Professor at the Universities of Aberdeen and the Witwatersrand. He sadly died, suddenly, in August 2020 — read our tribute.
