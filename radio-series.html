Radio series: amplifying voices of Community Health Workers

Community Health Workers are the interface between the community and healthcare services. South Africa adopted the Ward Based Primary Health Care Outreach Team (WBPHCOT) Strategy in 2011, and a five-year Policy Framework and Strategy was established in 2018 promoting community participation, empowerment and inter-sectoral collaboration. Implementation is constrained, however, and there is a call for CHW programmes to be regarded as a fully-fledged sub-system of primary health care rather than just human resources. This series amplifies the voices of CHWs to inform communities of their scope of practice and their role as the entry point to healthcare services.
