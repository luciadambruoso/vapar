The team

People

VAPAR has been built through sustained collaboration among communities, Community Health Workers, Mpumalanga Department of Health, the MRC/Wits Agincourt Research Unit and university partners. The people shown here have led different phases of research, facilitation, implementation, training, evaluation and policy engagement.

Founding leadership and legacy

Building the platform

PETER BYASS

Co-Principal Investigator, Professor, Umeå University

Peter Byass played a foundational role in the intellectual development of VAPAR and in the wider development of verbal autopsy methods. He died in August 2020, but his leadership, generosity and scientific influence remain part of the platform’s foundations.

VAPAR Mpumalanga

Verbal Autopsy with Participatory Action Research: a learning platform connecting communities, health workers, government policy and planning, and researchers to generate, act on and learn from research evidence on key local priorities.
