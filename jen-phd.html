Congratulations Dr Jennifer Hove

Community participation in health system strengthening: co-production of local evidence to influence action for health improvement in rural South Africa — awarded Doctorate in Public Health.

“It is with great pleasure that I present to you my PhD thesis. This work represents the culmination of years of research and study, and I am honoured to share it with you. Throughout this thesis, I have endeavoured to explore and contribute to the field of rural health, with a particular focus on community participation in health. My research has been guided by a deep passion for understanding the forms, processes, contexts, and mechanisms of community participation in health, and I have been fortunate to collaborate with many skilled and expert individuals within the Agincourt Health and Socio-Demographic Surveillance System site and along the way.

As you read through these pages, I hope that you will gain greater appreciation for the complexity and importance of community participation in decision making.” — Jennifer Hove PhD
