Postgraduate teaching and research

We work extensively in postgraduate teaching and research. Since 2013/14 we have hosted students from India, Nigeria, Ghana, Zimbabwe, the UK, Germany, Norway and Palestine, and continue to work with many of our graduates as they extend their professional roles. We also deliver a postgraduate taught course in health policy and systems research (HPSR), participatory methods and verbal autopsy at the Universities of the Witwatersrand and Aberdeen.
