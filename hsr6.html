VAPAR at the 6th Global Symposium on Health Systems Research

We contributed to the Sixth Global Symposium on Health Systems Research on re-imagining health systems for better health and social justice. VAPAR Project Coordinator Denny Mabetha presented a poster on strengthening social voices in health policy, planning and implementation — one of 100 of 600 posters chosen for verbal presentation.
